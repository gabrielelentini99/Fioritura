About Me

“A good piece of music is like a good dish of food: You taste it, you try to capture all the flavors that make it special and then enjoy it fully”.

This is how I approach every song I work on. I pay attention to every detail without losing sight of the big picture.

I have always been passionate about music since my early days in middle school. I learned from different genres and styles, from rock to jazz, from classical to pop. Natural born guitarist, I’ve recently discovered the world of musical post-production. I realized that there is so much more to music than just playing an instrument. There is a whole process of creating, editing, mixing and mastering that requires creativity and technical skills.

Eager to learn, in 2019 I decided to enroll in a degree program in sound engineering and music production.

I have a degree in audio engineering and currently I am pursuing a master’s degree at Ghedini Conservatory in Cuneo, Italy. There I have access to state-of-the-art equipment and facilities, as well as the guidance of experienced professors and professionals.

I work in Turin but I’m open to Remote Working as well.
